Can I use Selection Coach on a tablet or phone?

Selection Coach is fully compatible with Android phones and tablets (you must access Selection Coach through the Chrome, Firefox, or Opera app). iOS devices can access most of the features of Selection Coach, however, you cannot record audio or video on iOS devices. Frequently Asked Questions - Users

What is Example Mode?

Example mode allows you to listen to a model of how this conversation might optimally sound. You will hear both the customer and an actor portraying the part of the conversation you will be recording in Record mode. This will give you a model to compare your own recording to.

How do I register myself to Selection Coach?

Download the PDF here.

How do I delete my recordings?

Only company admins can delete recordings. As a work around, you can record over your old recordings to delete old ones. Only the most recent recordings are saved and accessible.

What if I have to stop or pause before I’ve finished a simulation? How do I save my work to that point?

Selection Coach will automatically save your recording when you click on the x to close the simulation. That way you can return later to complete your recording. You can press the 'X' in the upper right corner to exit.

Do I have to listen to the Example each time I work on a simulation?

We recommend that you listen to the Example the first time you work on a simulation so you have a model of what an optimal conversation sounds like. It is not necessary to listen each time. If you prefer, you can go directly to Record mode.

How do I submit my recordings?

When you are finished with the simulation, the last button will be a Submit button. When you press this and confirm you are complete, you will have submitted your recordings.
