How do I know if my video was submitted to my coach?

If your lesson has a yellow microphone next to it, that means your recordings were submitted to coach and your coach hasn't reviewed them yet. If there is a red or green microphone next to the lesson, your coach has posted feedback on the lesson to your mailbox. If there is no microphone next to the lesson, it has not been submitted to your coach. Make sure you hit the Submit to Coach checkbox in Practice mode to submit the recordings to your coach.

What is Example Mode?

Example mode allows you to listen to a model of how this conversation might optimally sound. You will hear both the customer and an actor portraying the part of the conversation you will be recording in Practice mode. This will give you a model to compare your own recording to listen for ways to enhance your vocal quality.

How do I send a message to my coach, Super Coach, or company admin?

To send a message to your coach, Super Coach, or company admin, your coach has to have send your a message first. If you receive a message from your coach, you can press the Reply button below the message in your mailbox to reply. Do not reply to email notifications as they are sent to an unmonitored email box.

What if I have to stop before I’ve finished a simulation? How do I save my work to that point?

Communication Coach will automatically save your recording when you click on the x to close the simulation. That way you can return later to complete your recording. You can press the 'X' in the upper right corner to exit. Then press 'Yes' to save. Press 'No' to delete your recordings.

Do I have to listen to the Example each time I work on a simulation?

We recommend that you listen to the Example the first time you work on a simulation so you have a model of what an optimal conversation sounds like. It is not necessary to listen each time. If you prefer you can go directly to Practice and listen to the Example by using that toggle buttons on the bottom of the screen. When you record it is helpful to compare yourself with the Example to listen for distinctions in tone, tempo, inflection and other vocal qualities. You find the way that works best for you to develop mastery.

Once I've finished a simulation - I can ignore it, right?

Think of Communication Coach like batting practice for a professional baseball player. Doing it once doesn’t develop mastery. The goal is to use each opportunity in Communication Coach to perfect a new aspect of your performance. You may do the same simulation multiple times for different reasons. The first time may be to get comfortable with the flow of the conversation. The next time you may be focused on the way to phrase information or ask questions. You may come back to it again to practice specific vocal qualities like tone or tempo. Each time is an opportunity to develop the “mental” muscle memory that ensures success on the job.

Why do you recommend only 15 minutes of practice time in Communication Coach?

Communication Coach is designed to be done in bite size sessions so you can fit it into your busy schedule easily. The recommended 15 minutes is what it typically takes to focus on one simulation, working through the process of Example, Practice using the toggle buttons to record and review and then visit the Coach screen for additional information.

I’m an experienced employee and am doing very well. What can Communication Coach do for me?

The great thing about Communication Coach is its flexibility. Each person can use the tool in the way that makes the most sense for them to continue to fine tune and perfect their performance. For you it may not be about “what” to say. Your focus may be on “how” to say it. Use Communication Coach to perfect all of the subtleties of tone, tempo, inflection and enunciation to ensure you are always projecting a confident, competent professional image through your voice. Remember, the best of the best in any endeavor – sports, music, etc, - practice their craft regularly to maintain mastery.

How do I submit my recording to my coach?

When you are ready to submit your recordings to your coach, press the checkbox above the workspace that says "Submit to Coach". The website will create a popup for you to confirm and if you are sure you want to submit, press 'OK'.
